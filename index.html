Trust is built into every step

Every constraint you mentioned is intentionally addressed in PulseRelief's design.

  • Rapid verification, not endless paperwork. Multi-source validation (partners, documents, metadata) runs in parallel for fast approvals. AI-powered document verification and real-time checks accelerate the process.
  • Instant, traceable disbursement. Whenever possible, funds go directly to service providers with a live, public ledger. Smart contracts enable automated milestone-based releases.
  • Radical transparency by default. Clear case status, verification badges, and release milestones visible to everyone. Blockchain-based immutable records ensure trust.
  • Interfaces built for stress. Single-screen flows, big actions, and plain language designed for people in crisis. Voice-based requests available for accessibility.
  • Global donor reach for local emergencies. Cases are auto-localized and distributed across multiple donor channels in minutes.
